Привет,
 
It's the result of more than a year worth of work: we are very happy to present you the new Open Food Facts mobile apps for iPhone and Android and to tell you how you can help us create an even more useful app with our crowdfunding campaign Better food choices for a better health, let's mobilize !
 
 
This blog post details the new features: continuous scan to get Nutri-Score and NOVA, simplified product addition, offline iPhone mode, Android product comparison and more !
 
And of course we do not intend to stop there, we have plenty of ideas to make the app even more convenient and useful. And you surely have some too! We would love to have your feedback: what do you like in the app? What could be improved? What functions would you find useful? To let us know, just reply to this email. :-)
 
Could you put a review for the new version of the app on the App Store and the Play Store ? If you've posted a review for a previous version, it is now much less taken into account into the average rating of the app, so it would be very useful if you could again put a (hopefully good) grade to the app.
 
Let's mobilize !
 
To accelerate the development of the next version of the app, we are launching the Better food choices for a better health: let's mobilize ! fundraising campaign today.
