If you have installed the Open Food Facts Aplikacija za iPhone, Android ali Windows Phone, tudi če je še niste uporabili za dodajanje fotografij izdelkov, ste morda že prispevali!




Indeed the simple fact of scanning the bar code of a food product is already for Open Food Facts interesting information: it allows us to calculate the popularity of the products. The more a product is scanned, the more popular it is. And when someone does a search on the site, we can offer the most popular products first.

We are now using the product scan information to determine in which countries the products are being sold. For example, if a product has been scanned by 3 different people in Belgium or Switzerland, then the product can be added to Open Food Facts Belgium or Open Food Facts Switzerland . It's a little robot named Scanbot who is taking care of it now. This morning he analyzed all the barcode scans and added 160 countries to 140 products, and he will now spend every day analyzing new scans and assigning new countries to the products.

This popularity information is also very useful to check in priority the information of the most popular products , to complete the incomplete product pages and to go on an expedition in search of products that have been scanned a lot but that nobody has added in the base.



Več skeniranih izdelkov imamo, več koristnih informacij zberemo, zato, če tega še niste storili, namestite našo brezplačno aplikacijo in jo pokažite prijateljem in kolegom, da jo tudi oni namestijo!

Scanning is good, and adding photos and info for missing products, it's even better! Once you have scanned a product, if it is not present in the database, thank you in advance to take a picture with the list of ingredients and the nutrition chart. :-)
