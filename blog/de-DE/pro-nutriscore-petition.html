PRO-NUTRISCORE-Petition zur Einführung des Nutri-Score in Europa
Die App und die Seite Open Food Facts berechnen und zeigen seit 2015 den Nutri-Score an, aber die Wirkung des Nutri-Scores wird noch größer sein, wenn er direkt auf der Vorderseite von Lebensmitteln angezeigt wird. No need to draw your phone to compare at a glance the nutritional quality of products. And the Nutri-Score will benefit everyone, including those less aware of nutrition!

The Nutri-Score on all products

Today the display of Nutri-Score on the front eds food products is optional. If you want Europe to make the display of Nutri-Score mandatory, you can sign the PRO-NUTRISCORE petition launched by 7 European consumer associations.



In addition to the Nutri-Score display on the Open Food Facts app ( try the brand new version! ), We've put tools in place to compare products based on their Nutri-Score .
