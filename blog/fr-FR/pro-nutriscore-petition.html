The app and the Open Food Facts site have been calculating and displaying the Nutri-Score since 2015, but the impact of Nutri-Score will be even greater when it is displayed directly on the front of food products . No need to draw your phone to compare at a glance the nutritional quality of products. And the Nutri-Score will benefit everyone, including those less aware of nutrition!

The Nutri-Score on all products

Today the display of Nutri-Score on the front of food products is optional. If you want Europe to make the display of Nutri-Score mandatory, you can sign the PRO-NUTRISCORE petition launched by 7 European consumer associations.



In addition to the Nutri-Score display on the Open Food Facts app ( try the brand new version! ), We've put tools in place to compare products based on their Nutri-Score .
