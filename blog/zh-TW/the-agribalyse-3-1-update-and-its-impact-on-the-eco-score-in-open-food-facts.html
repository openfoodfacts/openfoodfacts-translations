Agribalyse 的最新版本現在已經在 Open Food Facts 中更新！

自2000年代末以來，人們一致認為有必要對農產品進行環境標示。 Since the late 2000s, a consensus has emerged on the need for environmental labelling of agricultural products. In 2013, the first version of Agribalyse was jointly unveiled by the ADEME (The French Agency for Ecological Transition) and the INRAE (French National Research Institute for Agriculture, Food and the Environment).

Agribalyse ? What is it ? 這是什麼？

Agribalyse 於 2013 年啟動，是一項集體創新計劃，透過根據生命週期評估 (LCA) 方法建立的資料庫，提供 農產品和食品 對環境影響的參考資料。

自 2021 年 5 月以來，INRAE 和 ADEME 簽署了一項官方合作協議，並開放該資料庫。 The signatories of an official partnership since May 2021, the INRAE and the ADEME make this database available in open access. We have thus integrated these LCA results for the calculation of the Eco-Score in Open Food Facts.
Indeed, Agribalyse contains the LCAs of more than 2,500 product categories, which are the very basis of the Eco-Score calculation; having direct access to them guarantees a quick update of the data necessary to determine the Eco-Score.

此次更新帶來了哪些變化呢？

Agribalyse 3.0.1版本於 2020 年底推出，經過了增進和改良，將於 2022 年底推出 3.1 版本。

新版本包含更準確的資料，並改進了 Open Food Facts 上的許多環保評分，有助於進一步為感興趣的消費者、貢獻者和專業人士提供資訊。

全球超過 280 萬種產品中，有 35 萬多種產品採用了環保評分的計算方式。 The Eco-Score calculation is available on more than 350,000 products out of more than 2.8 million worldwide. Thanks to the data matching work done by Open Food Facts and initially by Data4Good, users have access to the environmental impact details of many products.

透過使用 Agribalyse 的環保評分方法，最大優勢在於能夠對產品的環境影響進行大規模評估。

This version of Agribalyse also contains new LCAs, and therefore the possibility to calculate the Eco-Score on even more products. It also allows for methodological, structuring and diffusion improvements, for instance, by expanding the product categories covered in version 3.1 compared to version 3.0.1. 此外，在方法、結構和傳播方面也有改進。例如，與 3.0.1 版相比，3.1 版擴大了所涵蓋的產品類別。
