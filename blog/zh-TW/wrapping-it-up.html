You have probably already seen codes such as "EMB 56251e" or "EN 38.012.001 CE" on food labels and packaging. And you probably did not pay any attention to them. And yet, these codes - called "packager codes" for the first and "European sanitary stamps" for the second - can teach us a lot about the products we buy and eat! 也可能從沒留意過。 也可能從沒留意過。 下圖的第一個編碼其實叫做「包裝代碼」，另一個則是「歐盟衛生章」，這些資訊都可以幫助人們更了解我們所購買、實用的商品。



每一個包裝代碼或衛生章都代表一家公司

The packaging code identifies the company that packaged the food product. This may be the company that made the product, or the company that imported it and packed it. 可能是製造產品的公司，也可能是進口和包裝產品的公司。

It must be mentioned when the name and address of the company that prepared the product is not clearly indicated on the packaging. Packaging codes are thus found on products where only the consumer service address of the brand is included. 只有標明品牌消費者服務地址的產品，才會有包裝代碼。

歐洲衛生章或歐洲衛生批准號會告訴我們生產或加工動物食品的公司。


現在，我們可以知道產品在哪裡製造、加工，或者至少可以知道包裝地。

The first 2 digits of the code "EMB 56251e" identify the department, here 56, Morbihan.
The next 3 digits identify the municipality where the company is located. But it is not the postal code, it is a number attributed to the commune by the INSEE. The number 251 of the french department of Morbihan is Theix. 但這三個數字不是郵遞區號，而是法國國家統計與經濟研究所（INSEE）給予的編號。 「251」代表的是莫爾比昂省的泰克斯鎮。

The codes of the form "FR 38.012.001 CE" correspond to the European approval numbers. The first 2 letters identify the country. And we find the number of the department and the number of the town in the first 2 groups of numbers. The last digit group identifies the company. 前兩個字母代表國家。 前兩組數字則是部門編號和城鎮編號。 最後一組數字則是公司代號。

看看各品牌的產品是不是產自同一家工廠

Another very interesting use of packaging codes is to cross-reference them across the entire Open Food Facts product base . It can be noted that some companies manufacture products for several brands. 這樣就能看到，有些公司會為多個品牌生產產品。

位於泰克斯鎮，代碼為 EMB 56251e 的公司也為 D'Aucy 品牌製造 青豆，並為 Cora 品牌製造扁豆。



另一個例子就是位於諾曼第 孔代鎮 的一家工廠，也生產 Elle & Vire 鮮奶油和家樂福半鹽奶油。

The fact that two similar products (for example, two boxes of lentils of different brands) are prepared or packaged in the same plant does not necessarily mean that the two products are identical. To make sure, you can compare the sheets of each product to check if the ingredients and the nutritional composition are also identical. 為了確保這一點，您可以比較各個食品標示，檢查成分和營養成分是否相同。
