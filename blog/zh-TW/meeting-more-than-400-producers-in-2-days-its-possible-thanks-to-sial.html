Meeting more than 400 producers in 2 days!? It's possible thanks to SIAL! 這得歸功於 SIAL！

For more than 40 years, the SIAL trade fair - Salon International de l'Alimentation - has been one of the world's largest events for all the players in the food industry. After a four-year absence due to the pandemic, SIAL was back from 15 to 19 October 2022 at the Parc des Expositions de Paris Nord Villepinte. 由於疫情的原因，SIAL 停辦了四年，將於 2022 年 10 月 15 日至 19 日在巴黎北維爾彭特的 Parc des Expositions 重新舉行。 

This year, for the first time, Open Food Facts decided to visit this fair to engage with food producers. And this, despite the transport strikes - that's how motivated we were 💪 雖然遇到了交通罷工，但我們依然鬥志高昂💪

我們可以從以下數據來瞭解 SIAL ： 

  • 240,000 平方米² 的展覽空間 (相當於32個足球場！) 分佈於8個展館 
  • 來自127個國家的7,280家參展商 (其中90%為國際參展商)  
  • 265,000名專業人士和參展者

那麼，Open Food Facts 為什麼要參加 SIAL 呢?

我們這兩天的主要目標是與國際生產商和製造商建立聯繫。

我們認為法國 Open Food Facts 產品資料庫的意識和發展已經相當不錯。 We consider that the awareness and development of the French Open Food Facts product database are now decent. To continue to increase our impact, we are therefore seeking to meet with food industry players in our neighbouring countries: Germany, Spain, Italy, etc., and thus establish our reputation in Europe as the food database referential.

There were no less than 800 Italian exhibitors at the show, so we were not disappointed with the trip! :-) :-)

在這兩天裡，我們特別著重於與參展商會面，但 SIAL 也是一個參與會議演講 (SIAL Talks) 、參加烹飪工作坊或調查 2030 年烹飪趨勢的好機會。 

我們這兩天的收穫 

  • 有很多義大利和西班牙的生產商參加展會
  • 越來越多的生產商把環境問題作為業務核心 (如肉類替代品、基於穀物的牛奶等) 
  • 多數法國公司知道並支持我們的使命 
  • The trend of food scanning applications is very specific to France. Very few international producers can name a scanning application. 但很少有國際生產商能說出這些應用程式名稱。
  • Open Food Facts 傳達的訊息大多受生產商歡迎。 The message carried by Open Food Facts is mostly welcomed by producers. And the only obstacle, occasionally encountered, is not related to the subject of transparency itself, but rather due to the lack of internal resources.

我們已經考慮到這些回饋，並明白我們在資料共享過程中需要發揮作用。 

首先，我們編寫了一份實用指南，幫助專業人士快速熟悉 Pro 平台，並在 30 分鐘內完成資料匯入！

Beyond that, we continue to work on improving the Pro platform to make it more functional and user-friendly. This will encourage even more producers to be more transparent. 這將鼓勵更多的生產商實現更大的產品透明度。 

如果您是生產商，並希望為更透明的食品系統做出貢獻，請寫信至 producers@openfoodfacts.org。

如果您是貢獻者，對從內部了解食品世界感興趣，並且對說服更多製造商分享他們的資料抱有熱情，請通過 producers@openfoodfacts.org 聯繫我們，或者直接在 Slack 的 #producers 頻道上聯繫我們。
