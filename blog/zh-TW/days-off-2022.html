The Open Food Facts Days 🔎🍊 is the community's annual event! 2 days to exchange, brainstorm, discuss but also share convivial moments. It's the perfect opportunity to get ideas flowing while having a good time together in the heart of Paris. 這個為期兩天的節日，我們能相互交流、集思廣益、討論議題，也能一起談天說笑。 這是在巴黎市中心共度美好時光、彼此交流的絕佳機會。

🗓 什麼時候舉辦？ 2022 年 10 月 1 日（星期六）到 2 日 （星期日）

📍Where ? in Paris, at the Académie du Climat. Address: 2, Place Baudoyer, Paris 4th arrondissement (between the Saint-Paul and Hôtel de Ville metro stations) and by videoconference (link to come). 地址：巴黎第四區博多瓦耶廣場 2 號（2, Place Baudoyer, Paris 4th arrondissement）（地鐵聖保羅站和巴黎市政廳站之間），會提供視訊直播（連結即將公布）。

To participate, register now! (Places are limited.) （名額有限）

星期六會專門舉辦由貢獻者、開發人員、使用者等計畫參與者所主導的說明會和研討會。

協會的 年度大會 將於 10 月 2 日（星期日）上午 10 點至中午 12 點舉行。 The General Assembly of the association will take place on the morning of Sunday 2 October from 10am to 12pm. And the afternoon programme will be free in order to be able to continue the exchanges and reflections on the points identified during the presentations, workshops and the General Assembly.

邀請大家共襄盛舉

Saturday's agenda is wide open: could you present what you do on Open Food Facts? Would you like to lead a workshop on a particular topic? And conversely, are there any topics you would like others to present? 你想主持關於特定主題的研討會嗎？ 還是你有希望其他人討論的主題？

你可以 將意見寄到 contact@openfoodfacts.org，也很歡迎你分享在 Slack 的 #journeesoff 頻道 （Slack 是貢獻者之間的討論平台， 快來註冊加入吧）。
