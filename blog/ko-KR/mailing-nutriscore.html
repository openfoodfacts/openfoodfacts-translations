The app and the Open Food Facts site have been calculating and displaying the Nutri-Score since 2015, but the impact of Nutri-Score will be even greater when it is displayed directly on the front of food products . No more need to grab your phone to compare at a glance the nutritional quality of products! And the Nutri-Score will benefit everyone, including those less aware of nutrition.
 
The Nutri-Score on all products
 
Today the Nutri-Score is optional. If you want Europe to make the display of Nutri-Score mandatory, you can sign the PRO-NUTRISCORE petition launched by 7 European consumer associations.
